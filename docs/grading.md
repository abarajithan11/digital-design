## In class quizzes

* We will track attendance and attention through in-class quizzes in each lecture and discussion.
* There will be 2-3 quizzes per session. They will be easy multiple-choice questions based on current/previous week material. 
* **You MUST answer all quizzes** (right or wrong) in every session for us to consider you attended that session.
* 10% of you grade will depend on correctness of your in-class quiz answers.

## Discord Participation

* We will have a discord server for this class, and we will create channels per lectures, discussions, assignment, exam preparation...etc.
* 5% of your grade depends on how much you engage with your peers and us on discord.

## Attendance

* In-person attendance (answering ALL quizzes) is mandatory for every session.
* Since this course is fast-paced, missing even a single session (= lecture/discussion) will make you quickly lose track and fail the learning outcomes.
* You may miss at most 2 session in-person.
  * If you do, within 4 days you should submit the printed set of slides, with handwritten annotations on each slide on what you learnt by watching the recording.
  * If you don't submit the notes, or if your annotations are not sufficient, you will lose up to 5% of the grade per day missed (10% for two days).
* If you miss more than 2 days, you will lose 5% will be subtracted from your grades **per day** missed.

## Assignments

Programming assignments will be auto-graded. We will run it through two testbenches, one that we give you and one that we keep secret. Your grades will depend on the number of test cases you pass and the elegance of your code.

## Final Exam

Final exam will be in-person, paper-based. Most questions will have SystemVerilog code related to your assignments. If you typed and debugged the assignments by yourself, you can easily get 100% in the final exam. Therefore, do not use AI for your assignments.

## AI use

* Since this is a beginner level course, any use of AI will impede your learning. 
* Once you learn the fundamentals (after this course), AI can be an excellent tool to boost your productivity.
* Avoid using IDE based AI for this course. You may ask targeted questions from chat-based online AI, if you really want to.
* If you do your assignments (design, write code, debug...etc) using AI, you will fail the paper-based exam worth 30% of your grade.
